Privacy Policy

Effective Date: May 24, 2026 · Last Updated: May 24, 2026

DiagnoseAI ("we", "our", "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights.

1. Information We Collect

  • Account Data: Your email address used only for authentication via Supabase Auth. Passwords are never stored in plaintext.
  • Vehicle Profiles: Year, make, model, and engine type you provide to improve diagnostic accuracy.
  • Diagnostic Data (DTCs): OBD-II fault codes retrieved from your adapter, stored in your private cloud profile.
  • Usage Analytics: Basic, anonymised session data used to improve stability. No personally identifiable information is included.

2. How We Use Your Data

  • To authenticate your account and display your personal scan history.
  • To pass anonymised fault codes to AI APIs for generating repair guidance.
  • To manage your in-app subscription through Apple and RevenueCat.

3. AI Processing

Raw fault codes and anonymised vehicle context are securely sent to Google Gemini AI for analysis. No personal data — email, name, or user IDs — is ever included in AI requests. Your data is never used to train AI models or for advertising purposes.

4. Data Security

All communication between the app and our servers is encrypted with TLS. Our Supabase database enforces Row Level Security (RLS), ensuring only you can access your own data. We do not sell your data to any third party, ever.

5. Third-Party Services

  • Supabase — Authentication, database, and Row Level Security. Privacy Policy ↗
  • Google Gemini AI — Anonymised diagnostic code analysis. Privacy Policy ↗
  • RevenueCat — Subscription and purchase management. Privacy Policy ↗
  • Apple App Store — Payment processing for Pro subscriptions.

6. Data Retention & Deletion

You may delete your account and all associated data at any time by emailing us. Upon request, your data will be permanently and irreversibly removed within 30 days.

7. Children's Privacy

DiagnoseAI is not intended for children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

8. Contact

For privacy questions or data deletion requests, contact us at support@diagnoseai.app.
