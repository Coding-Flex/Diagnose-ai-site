FAQ

Questions drivers ask before downloading.

Do I need an OBD-II adapter?

Yes. Diagnose AI needs a compatible OBD-II adapter to read fault codes and vehicle data from your car. The adapter plugs into the vehicle's diagnostic port.

Is this a replacement for a mechanic?

No. Diagnose AI provides informational guidance to help you understand codes and prepare for repair. Always verify safety-critical issues with a qualified mechanic.

Can the app tell me if it is safe to drive?

The app can provide practical urgency guidance based on the code, common causes, and symptoms. It cannot guarantee safety. If the vehicle feels unsafe, stop driving and get professional help.

Does every vehicle show the same live data?

No. Available data depends on the vehicle, adapter, ECU support, and connection quality. Some vehicles provide more sensor data than others.

How do I restore a Pro subscription?

Open the app, go to your profile or subscription screen, and tap Restore Purchases. App Store purchases are managed by Apple.
