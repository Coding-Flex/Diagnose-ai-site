Privacy Policy

Effective Date: May 24, 2026

At DiagnoseAI, we design systems that respect your privacy and secure your automotive diagnostic data. This policy details how your information is captured, processed, and safeguarded.

1. Information We Collect

  • Account Authentication: Registration email addresses and login credentials are encrypted and stored safely via **Supabase Auth**.
  • Vehicle Profiles: Custom details (Year, Make, Model, Engine Type) are kept to properly analyze fuel trim and diagnostic contexts.
  • Diagnostic Logs (DTCs): Raw fault codes parsed from your OBD-II adapter are stored securely in your private cloud profile to display your vehicle history.

2. Processing and AI Analysis

To turn raw error codes (e.g., P0171) into actionable DIY checks and urgency logs, we securely pass the code and vehicle context to our backend, which interfaces with generative AI APIs (Gemini/OpenAI).

  • No personal data, email addresses, or user identifiers are ever sent to the AI processing model.
  • Your diagnostic data is never sold or used for marketing profiling.

3. Security Architecture

All client-server exchanges are encrypted using TLS. Database tables are protected by rigid **Row Level Security (RLS)** in Supabase, ensuring that only you can access your vehicle's scan history.
